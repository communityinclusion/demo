Welcome to the Community Life Engagement Toolkit. We have a lot of great content for you, and you can use it in a number of different ways. You can read it online, in your web browser, using the arrows (or left/right keyboard keys) to navigate to navigate the toolkit in a linear fashion. Or you can take it with you. Download the PDF to read on your laptop (on or offline) or grab the Mobi or ePub version to read on your Kindle or iOS device respectively. This product is in beta, so bear with us while we work out any bugs.

What you'll find:
  • Engage Briefs presenting information on the four guideposts for Community Life Engagement, and our related research findings
  • Self-assessment tools to guide you in determining your areas of focus and next steps.
  • Promising practices in Community Life Engagement from service providers across the country.
  • Resources and tools for providing quality Community Life Engagement supports.


The five topical sections of this toolkit:
  • Background information on Community Life Engagement generally
  • Self-assessment materials to get you started thinking about your own CLE policies and services
  • Guidepost 1: Individualize supports for each person.
  • Guidepost 2: Promote community membership and contribution.
  • Guidepost 3: Use human and social capital to decrease dependence on paid supports.
  • Guidepost 4: Provide supports that are outcome-oriented and regularly monitored.
